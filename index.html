Frequently Asked Questions

Is our customer data secure?

Yes. Your data never leaves your infrastructure. We can install the system on your servers, or use secure cloud storage with encryption. You maintain full control.

Do you need access to our core banking system?

No. The system works standalone during customer onboarding. Once approved, you manually add customers to your core system (or we can integrate via API if you prefer).

What if the AI makes a mistake?

The system flags uncertain cases for human review. Only high-confidence approvals are automated. Your compliance team has final say on every flagged application.

How long does implementation take?

2-3 weeks from kickoff to live. Week 1: Setup & customization. Week 2: Staff training & testing. Week 3: Go live with support.

What happens when documents expire?

The system tracks expiry dates and sends automated reminders 90, 60, and 30 days before documents expire. Customers can submit updates through the same portal.

Can it detect fake Zimbabwe IDs?

Yes. The AI checks for common forgery patterns: photoshopping, wrong ID format, inconsistent fonts, tampered photos. It flags suspicious documents for human verification.
