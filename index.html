Frequently Asked Questions

Is our customer data secure?

Yes. Your data can remain on your infrastructure. We can install the system on your servers or use encrypted cloud storage; you retain control and access rights.

Do you need access to our core banking system?

No. The system works standalone for onboarding. After approval, you manually add customers to your core system, or we can integrate via API on request.

What if the AI makes a mistake?

Uncertain cases are flagged for human review. Only high-confidence approvals are automated. Your compliance team retains final decision authority.

What happens when documents expire?

The system tracks expiry dates and sends reminders 90, 60, and 30 days before expiry. Customers can upload updated documents through the same portal.

Can it detect fake Zimbabwe IDs?

Yes. The AI checks for forgery patterns (tampering, inconsistent fonts, edits). Suspicious documents are flagged for human verification with evidence and context.
